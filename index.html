The data has been taken from "The India They Saw" by Meenakshi Jain and Sandhya Jain which inturn describes relevant sections from 'Life', which is a biography of Huen Tsang

The total number of Sangharama-s stand at 1537 and Devalaya-s at 2251

The Sangharama-s which were deserted (marked with grey) are not counted since they were not functional anymore. At places where "many 100s" and such notations are used, these are just counted as 100, though they might be more or fewer in number

Some of the places have also not been identified, marked with (?) and will be updated with modern equivalents once the identification is done.
